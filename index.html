About Valley Hacks

Welcome to Valley Hacks

At ValleyHacks, we strive to bring together developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We usually try to keep things simple. Read our FAQs to phase out ambiguity.

FAQs

Who can participate?
Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. If you are under 18 years of age, we’ll need a parental consent form.
What if I don’t have a team or an idea?
Not to worry, most people don’t! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with. You can join our Facebook Group to find potential teammates.
What shouldn’t I bring?
Weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!
What about hardware?
If you have specific hardware in mind, bring it with you. We will be doing our best to lend hardware at the event and enable you to work on your dream hack. We’ll put out a list closer to the event so you can see what’s available.
I don’t want to hack, can I still get involved?
If you're looking to be a volunteer or mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), signup as a mentor below! If your company is interested in becoming a sponsor, reach out to us by signing up as a sponsor below.
When will applications close?
The application portal will be closed on March 4th at 11:59pm. We evaluate applications on a rolling basis and acceptances will be sent every weekend via email. The sooner you apply, the better chances you have of acceptance. Once accepted, you’ll have receive a form to confirm your attendance closer to the date of ValleyHacks.
How much does it cost?
Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to ValleyHacks is completely free, thanks to our sponsors! We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.
How big can a team be?
You can form teams of up to 4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.
Once I am accepted, what do I need to bring?
A valid government issued photo ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want and a geeky t-shirt!
What do I get by attending ValleyHacks?
Pretty much everything from socializing with the fellow hacker community to internship/job offers from the coolest tech companies. And of course there is the prizes we’ll have for finalist teams, as well as category-specifc prizes, revealed closer to the date of the hackathon. Most of all, you will gain an unparalleled learning experience and memories for a lifetime!
